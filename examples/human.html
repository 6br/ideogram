A human genome is depicted below. The grey and black bands in each chromosome represent heterochromatin. The pink region represents heterochromatin around the centromere ("pericentromeric heterochromatin"), and the blue represents variable regions.

This ideogram shows most chromosomes in a haploid state for simplicity. For more accurate, diploid representation of human genomes, see Ploidy, basic and Multiple, trio.
